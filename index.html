Biography

Education

I am currently in the midst of an exciting journey at JKUAT, where I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. As a dedicated learner, I am currently in my second year of study, actively exploring the vast and ever-evolving field of computer science.

Before embarking on this university adventure, I had the privilege of attending Butere Girls High School, where I completed my high school education. It was during my time there that I developed a strong foundation in academics and began nurturing my passion for computer science.

However, my educational odyssey commenced even earlier, at St. Lwanga Catholic School, where I laid the groundwork for my academic pursuits during my primary years. It was at St. Lwanga Catholic School that I first discovered my fascination with technology and its limitless possibilities.

These formative experiences have shaped me into the diligent and curious individual I am today. I am committed to expanding my knowledge, honing my skills, and embracing the challenges that come with a rapidly advancing technological landscape.
